About us

We make trail memories you can hold onto.

Trail Maps NZ was created for people who love New Zealand's outdoors and want to celebrate the places that mattered most to them.

Our story

We started with a single Great Walk map for a friend who had just completed the Milford Track. The response made it clear that there was a need for thoughtful, locally made trail art.

Today, we design 3D printed maps that balance accurate terrain with a clean, modern look. Each product is intended to feel personal, giftable, and display-worthy.

What we value

  • Local design inspired by Aotearoa's landscapes
  • Durable prints made for long-term display
  • Personal service for custom orders and framing
  • Respect for the places and stories behind each route
